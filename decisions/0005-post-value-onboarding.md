# 0005 — Account/community onboarding occurs after value

Status: partially resolved

After receiving a report, a visitor may create/link an Identity account, onboard or claim a community, attach the report, and later invite members.

Resolved:
- Identity owns the user account.
- The report remains available before authentication.

Unresolved:
- Which building owns community membership and invitations.
- Whether Score community registration and product community onboarding are the same noun.

No implementation may invent those owners.
